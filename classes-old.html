9 Week Term Art Classes (at COCA Gallery)
Five to six classes are provided each term. The cost is $165 per child for 9 weeks.

 
2008
 Term Theme
Term 1
"Art Naturally"
This term we are exploring art that uses materials that have been collected from the beach, the bush, and the garden! Make dyes from leaves and animals, from twigs and shells. Also, making the best use of the warm summer afternoons, we will sometimes take our drawing and painting to the art centre and the botanical gardens.
Term 2

"Colour Collision"
Making art that is bright, bold and colourful. Including all the children’s favourite art activities - using paint, paper, dyes, inks, collage, clay and crafts.

Term 3
 Imagine That!

"Imagine That!"
Paint glittering castles in the air, draw spooky scenes, and make a box full of mysteries! Art inspired by the wonderful world of your imagination! Including all of your favourite art activities using pencils, paint, paper, dyes, inks, collage, clay and crafts.

School Holiday (Boutique) Art Classes  (at COCA Gallery)
I provide morning (9.30am-12noon) and afternoon classes (1.30pm-4.00pm) during one of the weeks of the holidays. Each class has a different focus. Children can enrol for more than one class. There is a maximum of 10 children per class and I cater for ages 5 - 13 years. The cost is $30 per child.

Monday 21 April      1.30pm  –  4pm
PAINTING WITH MELTED CRAYONS

A children’s favourite!Paint a picture with hot melted crayons. Add a frame and take it home to hang on your wall.

 

Tuesday 22 April       1.30pm – 4pm
AN AFTERNOON OF MAKING COOL STUFF! YOU DECIDE WHAT!

Make a robot or box dog….create a mini bedroom makeover or a playground in a shoe box. Tables and tables of treasures to make things with, and lots and lots of glue to create exactly what you imagine.

 

Wednesday 23 April     1.30pm   –  4pm

MAKING FAIRIES AND GROTTOS

Make a fairy and a real fern garden with swings and seats for her to live in.

 

Thursday 24 April      1.30pm  –  4pm
CREATE WITH CLAY

Make a real hobbit house and garden with trees, a cave and a stream. For beginner and experienced clay sculptors  

 

Visiting Artist Classes
These are often single classes of 2 hours duration where I visit you to facilitate an art class. All materials are provided. (These classes have a teaching / preparation cost plus so much per person for materials. See Cost below).

Home Classes
I visit with all materials and run a 90 - 120 minute art project with your children and their friends.

After School Care or Holiday Programme (e.g. OSCAR)
I visit with all materials and run an exercise within your programme. This can be a one-off or over several days. Depending on the size of your programme each class can be structured around specific age groups.

Classes for Adults / Clubs / Social Groups
I visit your group and provide an activity for adults that is creative and achievable for all abilities. Each activity has an outcome that can be taken home by the individual.


Teacher Training / School Classes
Previously I have taught teachers through teaching their class of children. Teachers report being inspired to teach these classes themselves and have been surprised at how much the children have achieved in a two-hour class.

  • For example: Clay is a good topic as the materials are cheap and there are three simple principles that can make for a successful class.
  • Classes can be taught at the school or by visiting my classroom (at COCA)

 

Contact Helen for more details.

 

Cost
The cost of the Visiting Artist Classes vary depending on the art content, duration and the number of children. To give you a ball-park figure one class of 2 hours might cost $200 for the teaching / preparation, plus $2 - $3 per child for materials.

(Teaching / preparation includes use of my tools & resources, travel, preparation & purchase of materials, setup & cleanup, administration & planning).
