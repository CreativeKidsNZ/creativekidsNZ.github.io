Biography

Helen Savage graduated from Christchurch Polytechnic in 2001 with a Bachelor of Design (Craft Art), majoring in sculpture.

Helen's experience includes: conceptual design and crafting skills in drawing, photography, woodworking, graphics, ceramics, sculpture, printmaking, fabric construction, papermaking and welding.
