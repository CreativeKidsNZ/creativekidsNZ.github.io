Education:
"In 1996 I completed a full-time Foundation Art course as a prerequisite for getting into the Visual Arts degree course. I was awarded Most Promising Student at the end of the year and went on to do a Bachalor of Design (Visual Art). I taught art classes to adults and children and worked in galleries for eight years. In 2010 I completed a Post Graduate Diploma in Education at Canterbury University and began primary school teaching."
