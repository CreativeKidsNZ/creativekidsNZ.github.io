“CREATIVE KIDS” ART CLASS
FOR YOUR HOLIDAY PROGRAMME

The holidays are coming!
Choose one of the following workshops:

 

– Hobbit House and Garden –
This is a good introduction to working with clay and excites the imagination around model making, mythical creatures and natural materials. We will make a clay house and garden and decorate with a wide selection of natural materials such as shells and moss. The hobbit house and garden is on a cardboard tray and ready for each participant to take home.

 

– Gone Fishing –
A printing, drawing and construction activity in which each participant makes a fishing game (with 2 rods, a 'water' mat, fish and box) and takes it home to play with others during the holidays.

 

These activities suit all levels of skill and offer interest to both girls and boys from 5 – 12 years of age.

There are the times and activities available:

Friday 3rd Oct – Morning – Hobbit House and Garden
Friday 3rd Oct – Afternoon – Hobbit House and Garden
Monday 6 Oct – Morning – Gone Fishing
Tuesday 7 Oct – Morning – Hobbit House and Garden
