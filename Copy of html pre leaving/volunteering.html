VOLUNTEERING

Be part of an active teaching environment

Learn how to...

  • help children to discover their own creativity
  • set up an art room and activities
  • returning the classroom from chaos to order!...

 

 

 

 

 

 

Providing multi-media art classes is a labour intensive activity. There is the pre-class setting up of materials, like wedging and cutting the clay, cutting paper to the right size, laying the tables out, setting up a hand washing areas, etc. If you like being busy in a fun and playful environment, then helping out in the art classes might be just what you are looking for. Most of the activity happens in the one hour prior to the class and in the half an hour after the class.
