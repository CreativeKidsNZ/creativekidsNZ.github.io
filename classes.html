Holiday Programmes (e.g. OSCAR)
I visit with all materials and run an exercise within your programme. This can be a one-off or over several days. Depending on the size of your programme each class can be structured around specific age groups.

KiwiSport Holiday Programme Supervisor:
"Never before in the history of KiwiSport have I seen the kids so engaged for 3 hours in an art activity!"

 

Other Visiting Artist Classes
These are often single classes of 2 hours duration where I visit you to facilitate an art class. All materials are provided. (These classes have a teaching / preparation cost plus so much per person for materials).

Home Classes or Birthday Parties
I visit with all materials and run a 90 - 120 minute art project with your children and their friends. See Costs below).

Classes for Adults / Clubs / Social Groups
I visit and provide an activity for adults that is creative and achievable for all abilities. Each activity has an outcome that can be taken home by the individual. See Costs below).

School Holiday (Boutique) Art Classes
I provide morning (9.30am-12noon) and afternoon classes (1.30pm-4.00pm) during one of the weeks of the holidays. Each class has a different focus. Children can enrol for more than one class. There is a maximum of 10 children per class and I cater for ages 5 - 13 years. The cost is $-- per child.

I am currently not running these classes. Instead I am providing visiting artists art classes to holiday programmes  (see below).

 

Teacher Training / School Classes
Previously I have taught teachers through teaching their class of children. Teachers report being inspired to teach these classes themselves and have been surprised at how much the children have achieved in a two-hour class.

  • For example: Clay is a good topic as the materials are cheap and there are three simple principles that can make for a successful class.
  • Classes can be taught at the school or by visiting my classroom

 

Contact Helen for more details.
